Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both customers and drivers:

Free Cancellation

Cancelled more than 6 hours before scheduled pickup: Full refund — 100% of the fare is refunded to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Partial Refund

Cancelled between 2-6 hours before pickup: 50% refund — Half the fare is refunded. The remaining 50% goes to the driver as compensation for their time.

No Refund

Cancelled less than 2 hours before pickup, or if the driver has already arrived: No refund. The full fare is transferred to the driver.

Driver No-Show

If your driver does not arrive within 30 minutes of the scheduled time, please contact our support immediately. If we cannot arrange an alternate driver, you will receive a full refund within 48 hours.

Trip Interruption

If a trip is interrupted mid-way due to driver-related issues (vehicle breakdown, driver emergency), you will not be charged for the uncompleted portion. Contact support for immediate assistance and refund processing.
