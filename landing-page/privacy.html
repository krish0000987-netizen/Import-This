Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as information collected automatically when you use the Safar Go app and services.

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email address, profile photo, and payment information when you create an account.
  • Trip Details: Pickup and drop-off locations, travel dates, passenger count, and special requests.
  • Driver Information: Name, phone number, vehicle details, driving license, and verification documents (for driver accounts).
  • Communications: Messages sent through our app, support tickets, and feedback you provide.
  • Documents: Government-issued ID proof, address proof, and any other documents required for verification.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location during trips (with your permission) for tracking and safety purposes.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Trip history, search queries, feature usage, and interaction patterns within the app.
  • Call & SMS Logs: For verifying communications between drivers and customers (with consent).

Important: We only collect location data when actively using the app or during an active trip. We do not track your location continuously when the app is in the background.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To process bookings, match you with drivers, track trips, and facilitate payments.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and provide customer support.
  • Safety & Security: To verify drivers, monitor for fraudulent activity, and ensure the safety of all users.
  • Personalization: To recommend routes, save favourite destinations, and improve your experience.
  • Communications: To send trip updates, promotional offers (with your consent), and service notifications.
  • Analytics & Improvements: To analyse usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve our app and services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Drivers: For trip coordination, we share your name, pickup/drop location, and contact number with the assigned driver.
  • With Customers: Drivers' names and vehicle details are shared with customers for trip identification.
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us process payments, send SMS/email, provide cloud hosting, and offer customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • SOS/Emergency: In genuine emergency situations, we may share location data with emergency services.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored in secure, access-controlled data centres with encryption at rest.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorised personnel can access sensitive data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
  • Employee Training: All employees handling user data are trained on data protection and privacy practices.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining the highest standards.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy:

Account Data

Retained until account deletion + 90 days for legal compliance

Trip History

Retained for 3 years for billing and dispute resolution

Location Data

Retained for 30 days after trip completion, then anonymised

Driver Documents

Retained while driver is active + 2 years after deactivation

Support Communications

Retained for 2 years for quality and dispute purposes

Marketing Data

Retained until you withdraw consent + 30 days grace period

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. You can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Object: Object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@safargo.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the app to function — login sessions, security, and core features.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our app so we can improve it.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a better experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used (with consent) to show relevant ads and track campaign effectiveness.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our app may link to or integrate third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties:

  • Map Services: Ola Maps is used for navigation and route planning. Their privacy policy governs their data practices.
  • Payment Gateways: Payment processors (Razorpay, UPI) handle payment data. Review their privacy policies.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns.
  • Social Media: Links to social media platforms are governed by their respective privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes:

  • We will notify you via in-app notification and/or email at least 30 days before the changes take effect.
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
  • For significant changes, we will obtain your explicit consent where required by law.

Your continued use of the Safar Go app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
